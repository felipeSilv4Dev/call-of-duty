Biography

SAS Career

Johnny MacTavish, is the youngest candidate to pass Special Air Service selection in Royal Army history. He is known for "cleaning house" as a highly decorated officer under the tutelage of Captain John Price.

Task Force 141

After the death of Roman Barkov, and under the supervision of Kate Laswell, Price assembled a team called Task Force 141 and picked MacTavish as one of its members.

War Against Al-Qatala

Shortly after Task Force 141 eliminated Victor Zakhaev, preventing him from launching an ICBM towards the United States, MacTavish contacted Price requesting backup as things at his position are "heating up" outside Verdansk.
